This game is 8 bit game. Play with keyboard

Spacebar to startgame

Player 1
W walk up 
S walk down
A walk left
D walk right
H attack

Player 2
UP walk up 
DOWN walk down
LEFT walk left
RIGHT walk right
ENTER attack
